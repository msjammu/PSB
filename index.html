Welcome to Punjabi School Bothell

Empowering students of all ages by teaching Punjabi, Gurmukhi, and essential life skills while instilling the spirit of seva (selfless service)

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing and inclusive environment where students can learn Punjabi, Gurmukhi, Kirtan, and essential skills such as coding, finance, and public speaking.

We aim to foster a deep appreciation for Punjabi traditions, Sikh values, and the spirit of seva through high-quality education, engaging activities, and community involvement.

Our goal is to enable students to read and understand Punjabi with the aim of comprehending Gurbani in Guru Granth Sahib, ensuring that our heritage thrives for future generations.
