Path Forward for Winners

The Symposium is more than a local event ‚Äî it's the beginning of a journey!

Local

Participate at our Punjabi School Bothell chapter. All registered students present their speeches and are evaluated by judges.

Zonal / Regional

Top performers from local chapters across the region advance to compete at the zonal level against other SYANA chapters.

International

Regional winners are invited to the annual SYANA International Symposium, showcasing their knowledge alongside Sikh youth from across North America.

The experience is not just about winning ‚Äî it's about igniting a love for our heritage, building confidence, and forming lasting connections.
