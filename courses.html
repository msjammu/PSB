Earn World Language Credits

High school students in Washington State can earn up to 4 world language credits through Punjabi language proficiency testing!

Credits satisfy high school foreign language graduation requirements and may qualify students for the Seal of Biliteracy on their diploma. These credits can also be used for college admission requirements.
