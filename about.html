About Us

Building community through language, culture, and service

Who We Are

Punjabi School Bothell aims to provide a safe learning environment with a welcoming atmosphere which creates a sense of belonging amongst the families (sangat).

We maintain an inclusive environment which acknowledges and respects children from diverse family and cultural backgrounds. Our school strives to be at the center of the local community but we prepare our students with skills and mindset to contribute to the global world.
